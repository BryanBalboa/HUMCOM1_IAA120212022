ABOUT THE CAT CAFE

The Cafe was founded in Cat island by founder Ymir and backed up by the order of cats namely Battle cats or maid cats.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff we also offer free petts to serve as a tribute to the battle Cats.

"We together Strong." Collaboration with the help of the local cats it will make the place much more confortable.
